A letter for you 💌

Dearest, There are a thousand ways to speak, yet none seem enough when it comes to you. You exist in the quiet spaces of my day — in the pause between thoughts, in the softness of a passing breeze, in the way the sky lingers just a little longer before turning dark. Loving you feels less like a choice and more like something my heart learned on its own, slowly, gently… without asking me first. If I could, I would gather every unspoken feeling, every fleeting glance, every almost-word — and place them in your hands, hoping you’d understand the language my silence tries to speak. You are not just someone I love. You are the feeling I return to, again and again, like a familiar song I never grow tired of. And maybe I don’t say it enough — but somewhere between all the ordinary moments, you have become my favorite part of existence.
